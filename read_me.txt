This repository contains a report documenting the results of experiments performed in 2012.  These
experiments pertained to finding an optimal recipe for converting stale bakery waste into ethanol, suitable
for use as a fuel.  The experimental setup used the Feedstock Processing Controller, details of which are provided
on this site (https://github.com/BobGlicksman/FeedstockProcessingController).
